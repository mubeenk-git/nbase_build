Cross-platform EVM Apps

With our sophisticated Cross-platform Evaluation Module (EVM) apps, you can connect your EVMs, launch the app, and begin controlling and evaluating devices within minutes. Our apps, which have been specifically developed to connect EVMs to host systems(PC/Mac/Linux) via USB/Ethernet interfaces, can expertly handle low-level I2C/SPI data packet transmission to and from the EVM, assuring a seamless plug-and-play experience. Furthermore, any configuration changes performed on the app have an immediate impact on the EVM, demonstrating the device's real-time responsiveness to modifications.
