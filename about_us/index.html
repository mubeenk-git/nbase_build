About Us

nBase2 Systems was founded in 2018 with a clear vision - to provide groundbreaking technology solutions to the global market.

We are at the forefront of developing state-of-the-art mobility and IoT products for B2B enterprises and personalized electronic devices that transcend industry standards. We are also committed to engineering services for enterprises in Semiconductor, Audio, Speech, Communication, EVM Applications, SW Audio Tools, Web Technology development, Semiconductor-specific UI/UX tools, System Integration, and Test Automation.

Fuelled by a mix of experience and enthusiasm, we explore ideas that perfectly combine creativity with precision, establishing new industry milestones and redefining the digital landscape for the present and future.
