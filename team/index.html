Meet Our Team

Founders

Satish Kumar

With a tenure at Texas Instruments spanning from 1999 to 2017, Satish brings a wealth of experience in delivering solutions for embedded SoCs, Digital Signal Processors, and the implementation of Speech and Audio Processing Systems. A leader of global customer-facing teams, he conducted market studies and spearheaded the conceptualization and definition of a micro-mobility product tailored for 2-wheeler fleets.

Guhan Ganesamurthi

Formerly associated with Soliton Technologies from 2012 to 2018, Guhan brings extensive expertise in Web Technologies. He has an outstanding track record of developing products and tools in the Audio, Automation, and Semiconductor sectors, as well as leading development teams for micro-mobility technologies such as IoT firmware and cloud software solutions.


Senior Leadership

Naveen Sadashiviah

Having previously worked with Texas Instruments (1995-2012) and SanDisk/Western Digital (2012-2022), Naveen possesses many years of experience designing, executing, and delivering solutions for embedded SoCs, Digital Signal Processors, and NAND-flash-based storage, including SSDs. He takes pride in being at the helm of product execution, directing best-in-class products, and meeting the needs of key OEM customers.


Shobana Velusamy

Having honed her skills in Soliton Technologies, Shobana excels in developing software products at the forefront of web technologies for the IoT, semiconductor, and audio domains. Leading in the development of advanced and visually appealing user interface apps, she emphasizes quality and assures the timely delivery of great products to clients.



Sumit Nandi

Sumit, a seasoned Microsoft Windows Device Driver & Firmware Integration engineer, specializes in offering tailored solutions for consumer and enterprise-grade computing technologies. His expertise includes audio integration, AzureStack, testing throughout the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), and handling the complexities of firmware and driver interaction with a solid track record.
